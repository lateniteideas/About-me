Who is Marc?

Marc is a 25 year old illustrator, graphic designer and web developer.

And why illustration, graphic design and web development?

Since childhood, Marc showed interest in traditional illustration. Everything was fine until, in his teens, he discovered digital art and never again took a brush.

He started making fan made posters of his favorite movies even without knowing that other people were getting paid for doing that kind of things. Marc studied graphic design, he fell in love with this world and immersed himself in it for years. He created Lateniteideas and materialized all the ideas that were on his mind.

Years later he discovered the web development and, as he says, he felt this love again. Hooray for tech!, Marc adds.

What is Latenite Ideas?

Latenite Ideas it's a personal project where Marc manages all of his creative work -illustrations, UX/UI designs, merchandising, graphic design works for clients, etc- since 2016.

Does he work for free?

Nope, sorry.

Where does he see himself in 5 years?

Living where he likes, doing what he wants to do and working on his dream job. Where everyone should see themselves, he adds.

What does he like to do on his free time?

Well, -no one expects that, wait for it-, he loves to draw. He could work for 15 hours straight but, in his free time, he still wants to draw.

He also loves to exercise, play basketball, watch TV shows and movies and, as much as the next guy, having a beer with his friends.
